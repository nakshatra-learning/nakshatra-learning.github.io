How a PCB Is Made

What happens after you click "Export Gerbers" — the full manufacturing journey.

1

Data Prep

Customer data is converted into tooling — panelization, artwork, drill and route programs.

2

Imaging & Etch

Dry-film resist, UV exposure, develop and etch chemically pattern the copper layers.

3

Lamination & Drill

Cores, prepreg and copper foil bonded under heat & pressure, then mechanical drilling.

4

Mask, Finish & Route

Solder mask, HASL/ENIG finish, silkscreen legend and final board profiling.
